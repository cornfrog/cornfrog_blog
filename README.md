# Cornfrog_Blog

A personal digital garden and development log built with React, Vite, and Material UI.

## Overview
This project serves as a focused environment for documenting technical growth, architectural decisions, and the occasional intersection of life and labor. It is a space for "learning in public"—capturing the friction of building software before it becomes a polished tutorial.

## Philosophy & Intent
The blog is designed to prioritize clarity and speed. It exists to solve the problem of knowledge decay by creating a searchable record of solved problems and evolving thoughts. 

- **Not a Tutorial Site:** Content is focused on the *process* of development rather than providing step-by-step guides for others.
- **The Friction of Building:** Emphasis is placed on the breaking and fixing of things, rather than just the final state.
- **Dev + Life Mix:** Technical deep-dives live alongside reflections on burnout, career navigation, and the reality of being a developer in the modern world.

## Tags
Tags are intentionally limited to maintain high-signal categorization and prevent the organizational bloat common in personal blogs.

- `frontend`
- `backend`

- `debugging`

## Design & Focus
The UI is built around a customized, low-strain dark mode palette to ensure long-term readability. The project intentionally avoids over-engineering, keeping the focus strictly on two things: writing and building.
